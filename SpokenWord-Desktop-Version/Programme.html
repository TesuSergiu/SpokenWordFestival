18. AUGUST

OPEN WORD # 1 - M. CLAUS HØXBROE (OPEN SCENE)
TIME: 17-19
LOCATION: STUDENT ODENSE
ADMISSION: FREE
Open Word is the scene open and the word free. Beat poet Claus Høxbroe hosts.
Cozy atmosphere, relaxation among friends and peers, poetry slam, poetry, rap, performance art, linguistic and musical experimentation. Open Word is what you and I make it, so we know it will be good!

// Arranged by Spoken Word Festival and Student Odense in collaboration with Hans Christian Andersen Festival.

19. AUGUST

STORY ENTERTAINERS FEAT. MUSIKER ESBEN JUST
TIME: 20-23
LOCATION: THEATRE 95B
ADMISSION: DOOR: 60 / ENLARGE: 40/ BUY TICKET
Spoken Word Festival presents embarrassing, funny and grotesque stories from real life when Graversen and throat again guests Odense. This time invite the musician and songwriter Esben Just into a genremix of narrative and comedy accompanied by musician Øyvind Ougaaard.
The focus is on the good story when storyentertainer pair consisting of Henrik Strube and Gravers Graversen with musician Esben Just tell stories from real life accompanied by Ougaard on accordion. It is not music, it's not standup or just narrative - but something in the middle.
